Hi, my name is Dávid Kovácsvölgyi, let me introduce myself. I'm a software developer working/tinkering mostly with web technologies. I tend to view myself as a generalist since I try to focus on the craft itself, and not on one specific technology only. Maybe this is the reason that I like to tinker with new tools and technologies or try to re-implement already existing concepts. I created this website to share these projects themselves and my writings about them with wider audience.
